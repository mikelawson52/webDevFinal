About Us

Barking Beauty Dog Grooming has been located in Bethel, Ohio for over twenty years. Founded in 1996 by Melinda Boegeman, this store has been self-owned and self-ran its entire life. This means you'll get the best and most personal care towards your dogs. With a loyal and sizable local customer base, we have plenty of customers that vow there is no better place to groom your wonderful dogs!

Barking Beauty prides itself on offering competitive prices and excellent results. We are much more affordable and produce more quality results than other big name competitors.

We vow to make every dog feel at home and cater to your specific dog needs. We groom all types of dogs and accept any new customers, so come on in and book an appointment to make your dog shine!
